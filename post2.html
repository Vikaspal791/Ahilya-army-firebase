Ahilya Bai's Vision for a United India

Published on: June 12, 2024

Ahilya Bai Holkar was not just a ruler but a visionary who believed in the strength of a united India. She transcended regional and caste-based divisions, viewing all her subjects as one family. Her architectural contributions, from the Himalayas to the southern tip of the subcontinent, stand as a testament to her inclusive vision. The Ahilya Army Bharat Ekta Mission is committed to carrying this vision forward, working tirelessly to foster harmony and national pride.

Her philosophy was simple: a strong nation is built on the foundation of unity and mutual respect. She patronized art and culture from different regions, and her court was a melting pot of diverse traditions. By building temples and public amenities in pilgrimage sites across India, she reinforced the idea of a shared cultural and spiritual heritage that binds all Indians together. This message is more relevant today than ever before.

Our Mission

To unite all citizens of India under the banner of peace, harmony, and national progress, drawing inspiration from the principles of Lokmata Ahilya Bai Holkar. We are a non-political organization dedicated to social welfare, cultural preservation, and economic empowerment for all.

Declaration

We, the members of Ahilya Army Bharat Ekta Mission, solemnly pledge to uphold the sovereignty and integrity of India. We commit to fostering a society free from discrimination based on caste, creed, religion, or gender. Our mission is to build a self-reliant and prosperous India by promoting education, healthcare, and sustainable development. We will work tirelessly to empower the youth, uplift the marginalized, and protect our rich cultural heritage. Jai Hind, Jai Bharat!
